About us

Choosing Doggy Breaks will give your pets the opportunity to get fitter, healthier and happier by providing you and your beloved animals whether with a friendly, reliable and trustworthy service. We are based in Saddleworth which is where all dogs will be walked in open fields, along canals, countryside paths and never on the roads. We pride ourselves on genuinely caring for the pets we look after. All members of our team have long term experience with dogs and cats themselves. Your pets are in safe hands with Doggy Breaks.
